Trelby is collaboratively developed free software, and we welcome your participation! Listed below are various ways you can contribute, either via code, or interface design suggestions, or building services around Trelby. All Trelby relationed discussions happen on the Trelby list. Web interface: http://groups.google.com/group/trelby Subscribe email: trelby-subscribe@googlegroups.com Code Below are some of the changes in our horizon. Pick one up here, or scratch your own itch! Add “scene” list to the layout. Add support for bold and italic formatting of text. Add outline/index card capability. Add .fdx and .html export options. Add .fdx import capability. Move note taking from within the page to outside the page border. Enhance spell checking. Mark unidentified words, and popup suggestions on right click. Add dual-dialog support. Mobile Are you an Android or iOS developer? Help us build a simple Android and iOS for Trelby. Interface Trelby aims to be the simplest and smartest screenwriter. We want the user to forget that he’s using Trelby, and get out of his way as fully as possible. We also want to provide the maximum assistance we can to the process of forming, and writing a story.
